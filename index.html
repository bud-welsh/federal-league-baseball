Federal League Baseball

This site is a tribute to Federal League Baseball. During the 1914 and 1915 seasons, the FLB was considered the third major league in baseball, along with the National League and American League.

1913

The Federal League was started in 1913 by John T. Powers as a minor league playing as an "outlaw" league outside of the National Agreement.
